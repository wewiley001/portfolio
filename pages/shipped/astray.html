Astray: A Forsaken Tale

Astray is a first-person dark fantasy/horror stealth game where the player takes on the role of Bridgitte, a young girl whose castle has been overtaken by an evil force and corrupted her once-loyal servants. Without any means to fight, she sneaks through her home in search of her parents while uncovering the mysteries behind the madness.
