Cyberpunk Apartment

Game Development Level/Environment Unreal Engine

Background

Over several years, I created a series of virtual backdrops within Unreal Engine to be used against a greenscreen or within a live studio feed. All assets were sourced from free/CC places online. The cityscape seen outside the windows is a video capture of the Night City skyline from Cyberpunk 2077. Neon lighting elements were made in-engine with custom materials. The environment featured above was made in Unreal Engine 5.
