Core Manor Extension (WIP)

  • Role: Mod Creator
  • Game: Starfield

I created an additional space in one of the players' homes because I felt the place was lacking a lot and needed a basement/underground area, like many other places in the settlement. There's placeholder/vanilla clutter around for demoing purposes.

Future plans include building out a more substantial work area in the basement for crafting, and a remote ship management system for upgrades and purchases.
