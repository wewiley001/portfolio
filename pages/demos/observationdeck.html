Obervation Deck Interior

Game Development Level/Environment Unreal Engine

Background

This environment was created in Unreal Engine 5 using free assets, in-engine lighting, and particle effects. Blueprint was used to manage and maintain queued lighting effects. The setting is intended to be an observation deck of a space station orbiting a planet, but it is being used sloppily as a storage area.
